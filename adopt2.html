Adopt a pet Stress-Free


We make adopting a pet easy and stress-free both for you and your pet.

PawStop , India's first pet care market place has taken the initiative to moke it feel home . We have created a reliable, simple & free initiative to help you by providing some tips.

Here are some tips for putting your pet for adoption a pup or a kitten -

Make your pet look more attractive to potential new owners. Make sure your pet is vaccinated and checked by a veterinarian. Also, make sure your pet is spayed and neutered. This will make your cat or dog more likely to be chosen by a new owner.

Be transparent/clear with the new owner. Share all the details about your pet's personality and how they get along with cats, dogs, and people. Share your dog's or cat's favorite food, game, things, and any medical or behavior issues your pet is experiencing so that potential new adopters will have the information they need to determine if your cat or dog would be a good fit for their home and family.


Why adopt a pet?


  • Save a life by giving a shelter animal a home.
  • Provide every detail of your pet including diet, medication, or existing illness if any.
  • Help reduce the problem of animal overpopulation.
  • Experience the fulfillment of making a positive impact.
  • Build a special bond that enriches your daily life.

Pawstop is India's first pet care marketplace to provide an online pet adoption platform and community where people who want to adopt a dog or cat connect with people who need to rehome a dog or cat. The best possible way to care about your beloved pet is to keep them out of the shelter homes and rehoming your dog or cat to a loving family. Rehoming a dog or cat is the best kind of pet adoption, in every possible way.
