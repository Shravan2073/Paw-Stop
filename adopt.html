Your Pet Adoption Journey With PawStop

Adopt a dog or cat who's right for you. Simply enter your city above to start your search.
Once you find a pet, click "show number" to get contact info for their pet parent or rescue. Contact them to learn more about how to meet and adopt the pet.
The rescue or pet parents will walk you through their adoption process. Prepare your home for the arrival of your fur baby to help them adjust to their new family.
Pawstop will help your pet to settle down in its new home, once you complete the Adoption journey reach out to us for free vet consultation.

#AdoptLove

Approximately 1478 dogs & cats die every day on road in India. PawStop is on a mission to provide every dog and cat a home before 2035. It’s just one of the many ways PawStop gives back and helps you become a part of something larger. Join ThePetStar Community and help setting up Pet houses in your surrounding for strays.

Adopting a pet is a profoundly touching experience that fills your life with boundless love and heartfelt companionship. Imagine the moment when a homeless animal, longing for a family, looks into your eyes with hope and gratitude. By choosing adoption, you are not just giving a pet a home; you are saving a life and making a forever friend who will cherish every moment with you. The joy of watching a once-shelter animal blossom under your care is indescribable, and the bond you form is unbreakable. Every wag of the tail, purr, or affectionate nuzzle is a testament to the incredible love and loyalty that only a rescued pet can offer. Adopting a pet is a beautiful act of kindness that enriches both your life and the life of a grateful animal, creating a story of redemption and unconditional love that will touch your heart every single day.
